Syntax: ./main <command> <num1> <num2>
commands: 
    add  -  adds 2 numebrs
    sub  -  subtrackts the 2nd from the 1st number
    mul  -  multiplys 2 numbers
    div  -  divides the 1st by the 2nd
    pow  -  adds the 1st number to the power of the 2nd

Examples:              outputs:
    ./main add 4 5         9.00
    ./main sub 10 2        8.00
    ./main mul 2 4         8.00
    ./main div 10 3        3.33
    ./main pow 3 2         9.00
